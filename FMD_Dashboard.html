Pork industry view

SAPPO: Pig sector vaccine dispatches.

Submitted by the South African Pork Producers' Organisation (SAPPO). Covers Aftodoll (Dollvet) vaccine dispatched by SAPPO representatives to pig producers across South Africa. Some bottles were also used for cattle on the same farms to reduce cross-species FMD risk — the cattle/pig split is not available at dispatch level. Source: email from Dr Thandi Chiappero, SAPPO Head: Consumer Assurance, 3 June 2026.
